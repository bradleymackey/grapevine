Your privacy is important to us.

It is Grapevine's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our app. Accordingly, we have developed this privacy policy in order for you to understand how we collect, use, communicate, disclose and otherwise make use of personal information. We have outlined our privacy policy below.

  • We will collect personal information by lawful and fair means and, where appropriate, with the knowledge or consent of the individual concerned.
  • Before or at the time of collecting personal information, we will identify the purposes for which information is being collected.
  • We will collect and use personal information solely for fulfilling those purposes specified by us and for other ancillary purposes, unless we obtain the consent of the individual concerned or as required by law.
  • Personal data should be relevant to the purposes for which it is to be used, and, to the extent necessary for those purposes, should be accurate, complete, and up-to-date.
  • We will protect personal information by using reasonable security safeguards against loss or theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification.
  • We will make readily available to customers information about our policies and practices relating to the management of personal information.
  • We will only retain personal information for as long as necessary for the fulfilment of those purposes.

We are committed to conducting our business in accordance with these principles in order to ensure that the confidentiality of personal information is protected and maintained. Grapevine may change this privacy policy from time to time at Grapevine's sole discretion.

Services Used by Grapevine

Grapevine makes use of a number of third party services in order to operate a set of distinct features and process data. These third party services operate under their own privacy policies. By agreeing to Grapevine's privacy policy you are also agreeing to these third party vendor's privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics, provided by Google Inc. (How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps, Privacy Policy). We use Google Analytics to better improve Grapevine by anonymously collecting some usage data. The anonymous usage data is not linked to any personally identifying information. The handling of identifiers for mobile devices (including Android Advertising ID and Advertising Identifier for iOS), is managed by the Google Analytics SDK. Google may use this identifier to deliver personalised advertisments within their advertising network. As this analytics data is required by Grapevine to improve our service, this cannot be disabled. If you do not want anonymous usage data to be collected about you, do not use Grapevine.
  • Facebook Auth, Graph, Audience Network, provided by Facebook Inc. (Privacy Policy). Grapevine only processes name and ID data for the authenticated user and their Friends whom have also authenticated Grapevine. Some friend data is saved to our servers in order for Grapevine to function as intended. Identifiers for mobile devices (including Android Advertising ID and Advertising Identifier for iOS) may be collected by the Facebook Audience Network in order to display targeted advertisments within their advertising network.
  • OneSignal, owned and operated by Lilomi, Inc. (Privacy Policy). Grapevine uses OneSignal in order to send push notifications. In order for these to work as intended you have a notification identifier which is saved to our servers and this is used to send you personalised push notifications. If you have 'Friend Notifications' enabled within Grapevine, every message you send will also contain this identifier. Your identifier could potentially be used to identify you if required by law. If you do not want your identifier to be known, do not use Grapevine. The OneSignal SDK collects some information about you in order for the notifications to function properly and to improve their product. Please see OneSignal's privacy policy to see exactly which data may be collected about you. If you do not want this data to be collected about you, do not use Grapevine.

Legal Action

Grapevine may be required to comply with law enforcement agencies in the event of misuse of the Grapevine service or related incidents. In the event of this, we will fully comply with law enforcement providing any information we have at our disposal. Such legal action may result from the creation, propagation or encouragement of objectional content or abuse on the Grapevine platform.

Data Breaches

In the unlikley event of a data breach, users will be notified within 1 week of this being confirmed via a push notification and notice on our website Grapevine.pro.
